### **Universal Pillars of English Essay Marking Criteria
#### 1. Content and Analysis (30%)
- **The Thesis**: Direct Answering
- **Evidence**: Source citations
- **Critical Thinking**: Relevance
#### 2. **Structure and Organization (20%)
- **Introduction**: Catchy start
- **Paragraphs**: Topic Division
- **Transitions**: Proper use of “However,” “Furthermore,” and “Consequently”
#### 3. **Language and Vocabulary (25%)
- **Academic Tone**: Avoid slang or “text speak.”
- **Word Choice**: Using specific words instead of general ones (e.g., “beneficial” instead of “good”).
#### 4: **Grammar and Accuracy (25%)
- SPaG (Spelling, Punctuation, and Grammar)
