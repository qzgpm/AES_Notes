- **Natural Language Processing** (NLP) helps machines to understand and process human languages either in text or audio form.
- It is used across a variety of applications from speech recognition to language translation and text summarization.


### NLP Techniques:
#### 1. **[[Text Normalization]]:**
- Case Conversion (Lower Case)
- Remove Number
- Remove Non-Alphabets
- Remove Extra White Spaces
- Remove Stop Words
#### 2. **[[Tokenization]]:**
- Individual ***Words*** -> ***Tokens***
- Individual ***Sentences*** -> ***Tokens***
#### 3. **[[Stemming]]:**
- Removing Affixes
